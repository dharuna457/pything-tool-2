How to use:

1. Upload a **certificate template image** (PNG or JPG). This will be the background for your certificates.

2. Upload a **CSV file** with your certificate data. It **must** have columns named `Name`, `Course`, and `Date`.

Example CSV content:

Name,Course,Date
